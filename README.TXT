INTRODUCTION
------------

Khet is a chess-like abstract strategy board game involving lasers and various pieces with mirrors.  
Players take turns moving Egyptian-themed pieces around the playing field, firing their laser after each move. 



Controls
--------

Left-Click on mouse = counter-clockwise turn of piece
Right-Click on mouse = clockwise turn of piece
Mouse drag = move piece to desired square
